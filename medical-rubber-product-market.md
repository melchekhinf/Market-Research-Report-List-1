<p><p>The medical rubber product market can be classified into two types: natural rubber products and artificial rubber products. Natural rubber products are made from the sap derived from rubber trees and have superior elasticity and biocompatibility. They are widely used in medical gloves, catheters, and surgical instruments. On the other hand, artificial rubber products are synthetic materials that mimic the properties of natural rubber. They are cost-effective and offer excellent durability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for applications such as tubing, seals, and implants in the medical field.</p></p>
