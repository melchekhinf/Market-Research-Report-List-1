<p><p>Camellia oil is available in two main market types: extra virgin camellia oil and pure camellia oil. Extra virgin camellia oil is obtained through a cold-pressing process, ensuring the oil retains its natural flavor and nutritional qualities. It is considered the highest grade of camellia oil, known for its delicate taste and aroma. Pure camellia oil, on the other hand, is obtained through refining or a combination of refining and cold-pressing methods. It has a milder flavor and is widely used for cooking, skincare, and hair care purposes.</p></p>
