<p><strong>The Ceramic Fiber Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ceramic Oxide Fiber</li><li>Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Fiber Fabric market can be divided into two types: Ceramic Oxide Fiber and Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber. Ceramic Oxide Fiber is made from ceramic materials that contain oxygen, such as alumina and silica. It offers excellent thermal resistance and is commonly used in applications requiring high-temperature insulation. On the other hand, Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber is made from ceramic materials that do not contain oxygen, such as silicon carbide and boron nitride. It provides exceptional strength and stiffness, making it suitable for applications requiring mechanical durability and structural reinforcement.</p></p>
