<p><p>Pallet wrapping film is used for securing and stabilizing palletized goods during transportation. There are different types of pallet wrapping film available, namely cast film, blown film, pre-stretch film, and others. Cast film is made using a cast extrusion process, which ensures excellent clarity and quiet unwinding. Blown film is produced by forcing air into a melt to form a bubble, resulting in a stronger and more puncture-resistant film. Pre-stretch film is stretched before application, reducing the amount of film needed and providing cost savings. Other types may include specialty films like colored films or UV-resistant films.</p></p>
