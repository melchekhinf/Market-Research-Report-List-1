<p><p>UV curable resin for optical discs market is segmented into two types: Radical UV-curable resin and Cationic UV-curable resin. Radical UV-curable resin uses free radicals to initiate the curing process, while Cationic UV-curable resin relies on cations. These resins are used in the production of optical discs such as CDs and DVDs. Radical UV-curable resin offers faster curing times and better adhesion, while Cationic UV-curable resin provides better chemical resistance and stability. Both types cater to different customer requirements and have their own advantages in the market.</p></p>
