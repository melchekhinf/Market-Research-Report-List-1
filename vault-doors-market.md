<p><p>Vault doors are specialized doors designed to provide enhanced security for high-value assets. Out-swing vault doors refer to doors that open outward, away from the secure area, making it easier to enter and exit the vault space. They are suitable for applications where limited space inside the vault is available. In-swing vault doors, on the other hand, open inward towards the secure area, requiring more space, but providing added security against potential forced entry. The choice between out-swing and in-swing vault doors depends on the specific requirements and spatial constraints of the location.</p></p>
