<p><p>Prism compressors are used to compress laser pulses in optical systems. Prismatic pulse compressors consist of a single prism that separates the laser pulse into different colors. The different colors then travel through a dispersive medium and recombine to form a shorter pulse. Multiple-prism pulse compressors, on the other hand, use multiple prisms arranged in a specific geometry to achieve higher compression ratios. This allows for even shorter pulse durations, making them suitable for various applications in laser technology and scientific research.</p></p>
