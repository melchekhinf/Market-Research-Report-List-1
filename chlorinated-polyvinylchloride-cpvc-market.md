Solid-phase method CPVC is manufactured by direct chlorination of PVC resin in a solid state. Aqueous suspension method CPVC is produced by suspending PVC resin particles in water and then chlorinating the suspension. Other CPVC types may include variations obtained through different processes or formulations.</p></p>
