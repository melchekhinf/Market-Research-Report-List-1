<p><p>Solid zirconia is a highly durable material used in dental restorations like crowns and bridges. It is classified into Type I and Type II based on its translucency and applications. Type I zirconia is less translucent and suitable for posterior restorations where strength is prioritized over aesthetics. Type II zirconia has higher translucency and is used for anterior restorations where both strength and aesthetics are important. These market types help dentists choose the right zirconia material based on their patients' requirements, ensuring optimal results in dental restorations.</p></p>
