<p><strong>The Circular Finned Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Metal Finned Tubes</li><li>Bimetal Composite Finned Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the circular finned tube market, there are two main types: single metal finned tubes and bimetal composite finned tubes. Single metal finned tubes are made of one type of metal throughout, with fins attached to the tube surface to increase heat transfer efficiency. On the other hand, bimetal composite finned tubes are made by bonding two different metals together, combining the advantages of both metals in terms of heat resistance and mechanical strength. These types of tubes are commonly used in various industries for heat exchangers and air coolers.</p></p>
