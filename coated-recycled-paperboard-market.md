<p><p>Coated recycled paperboard refers to a type of paperboard that has a coating applied to its surface to enhance its printing and packaging capabilities. There are three main types of coatings used in this market. Kaolin clay, also known as China clay, provides a smooth and glossy surface to the paperboard. Titanium dioxide is a white pigment that gives brightness and opacity to the coating. Calcium carbonate is used as a filler to increase the bulk and stiffness of the paperboard. These coatings are widely used in various industries for packaging and printing purposes.</p></p>
