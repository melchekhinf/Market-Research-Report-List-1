<p><strong>The Veterinary ECG Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Holter ECG</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veterinary ECG systems are used to monitor and diagnose heart conditions in animals. Resting ECGs are conducted while the animal is at rest, providing a baseline reading. Holter ECGs are done over a 24-hour period, monitoring the heart's activity throughout the day. These systems can detect irregular heart rhythms and abnormalities. Other types of veterinary ECG systems include telemetry ECGs, which can remotely monitor the heart's electrical activity, and stress ECGs, which are performed during physical exertion. These different types cater to specific needs in diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions in animals.</p></p>
