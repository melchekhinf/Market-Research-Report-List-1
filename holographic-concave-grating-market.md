<p><p>The Holographic Concave Grating Market consists of two types: Transmission Grating and Reflective Grating. Transmission Grating is a device that allows light to pass through, diffracts it, and produces a spectrum. It is often used in applications such as spectroscopy and laser systems. Reflective Grating, on the other hand, reflects light at different angles, creating a diffraction pattern. It is widely used in applications like monochromators and wavelength division multiplexing. Both types serve important roles in various fields, offering different functionalities based on light transmission or reflection.</p></p>
