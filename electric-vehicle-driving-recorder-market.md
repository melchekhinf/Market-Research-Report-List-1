<p><ul><li>Integrated</li><li>Portable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric vehicle driving recorder market can be categorized into two types: integrated and portable. Integrated driving recorders are built-in devices usually installed during the manufacturing of electric vehicles. They are seamlessly integrated into the vehicle's system and offer continuous monitoring and recording of driving data. On the other hand, portable driving recorders are external devices that can be moved between vehicles. They provide flexibility and convenience as they can be easily installed and removed. Both types of recorders serve the purpose of monitoring and recording driving activities, but differ in their integration and portability features.</p></p>
