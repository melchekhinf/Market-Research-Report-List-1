<p><p>The fuel delivery system market includes various types of components such as fuel pumps, injectors, pressure regulators, filters, fuel rails, air control valves, and throttle position sensors. Fuel pumps are responsible for supplying fuel to the engine, while injectors ensure the proper amount of fuel is delivered for combustion. Pressure regulators maintain consistent fuel pressure, ensuring efficient engine performance. Filters remove impurities from the fuel, enhancing its quality. Fuel rails transport fuel to the injectors, while air control valves regulate the air-fuel mixture. Throttle position sensors monitor throttle position to adjust fuel delivery accordingly.</p></p>
