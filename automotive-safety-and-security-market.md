<p><ul><li>Active Safety</li><li>Passive Safety</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive safety and security market is categorized into active safety and passive safety. Active safety focuses on preventing accidents by using technologies like anti-lock braking systems, electronic stability control, and adaptive cruise control. These systems help in maintaining control over the vehicle and avoiding collisions. On the other hand, passive safety refers to techniques that mitigate the impact of an accident or collision, such as seat belts, airbags, and crash sensors. These measures aim to minimize injuries and protect the occupants once an accident has already occurred.</p></p>
