<p><p>ANC (Active Noise Cancelling) headphones are available in three market types: In Ear, On Ear, and Over Ear. </p><p>In Ear headphones are small and lightweight, fitting snugly into the user's ear canal. They provide a compact and portable audio solution, suitable for individuals who prefer a more discreet option.</p><p>On Ear headphones rest on the outer ear and provide a comfortable and immersive listening experience. They are larger than In Ear headphones but still relatively compact and offer a good balance between portability and sound quality.</p><p>Over Ear headphones enclose the entire ear, providing excellent noise isolation and optimal sound quality. They are typically larger in size, making them great for home use or for those seeking a truly immersive audio experience.</p></p>
