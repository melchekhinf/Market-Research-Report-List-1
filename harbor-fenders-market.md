<p><p>Harbor fenders are used to protect ships and docks from damage during mooring operations. There are several types of harbor fenders available in the market. Solid rubber fenders are durable and provide excellent shock absorption. Pneumatic fenders are inflatable and offer high energy absorption. Foam fenders are lightweight and resistant to corrosion. Timber fenders are made from wood and are commonly used for smaller vessels. Each type of fender has its own advantages and is chosen based on various factors such as vessel size, impact forces, and port requirements.</p></p>
