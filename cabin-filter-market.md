<p><p>Cabin filters are of two types: normal type and activated carbon series. The normal type cabin filters are basic filters that remove dust, pollen, and other particles from the air entering the cabin. On the other hand, activated carbon series cabin filters not only filter out dust and pollen but also have a layer of activated carbon that can effectively absorb harmful gases and smells, providing better air quality inside the vehicle. Both types of filters are commonly used to enhance the air filtration system in cars and improve the overall comfort and health of occupants.</p></p>
