<p><p>The polyethylene lidding films market consists of three types: single layer films, dual layer films, and multilayer films. Single layer films are made up of a single layer of polyethylene, providing basic protection and sealing for packaging applications. Dual layer films have two layers, offering improved barrier properties and heat resistance. Multilayer films combine multiple layers of polyethylene, adding enhanced barrier protection, puncture resistance, and optimal sealing properties. Each type offers different levels of performance and functionality, catering to various packaging needs in the market.</p></p>
