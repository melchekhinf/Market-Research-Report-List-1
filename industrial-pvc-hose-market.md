<p><p>The industrial PVC hose market consists of three main types: PVC non reinforced hose, PVC fibre reinforced hoses, and PVC steel wire hose. PVC non reinforced hose is a basic type that lacks additional strengthening materials. PVC fibre reinforced hoses have a layer of fabric or fibre embedded in the hose wall to enhance strength. PVC steel wire hose has a steel wire helix embedded in the wall for added durability and resistance to pressure. Each type serves different industrial applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
