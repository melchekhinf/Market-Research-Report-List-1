<p><p>Direct-driven wind turbine generator market consists of two types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to the traditional direct-driven wind turbine generator that is widely used in the industry. It consists of a gearbox that connects the low-speed rotor to a high-speed generator, ensuring optimal energy conversion. On the other hand, Type II represents a newer technology where the gearbox is eliminated, resulting in a lighter and more compact design. This type offers reduced maintenance requirements and potentially higher efficiency, making it an attractive option for wind energy developers.</p></p>
