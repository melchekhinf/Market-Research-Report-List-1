<p><p>An Intrusion Detection and Prevention Tool (IDPT) is a software or hardware solution designed to protect computer systems and networks from unauthorized access or attacks. Once detected, IDPTs can take proactive measures to prevent or stop the attack, such as blocking or diverting traffic, notifying administrators, or initiating automated responses.</p><p>The market for IDPTs has been witnessing steady growth in recent years due to the increasing number and complexity of cyber-attacks. Organizations across various sectors, including banking, healthcare, government, and IT, are realizing the importance of investing in robust security solutions to protect their sensitive data and infrastructure.</p><p>Factors driving the growth of the IDPT market include the rising adoption of cloud-based services, the growing popularity of IoT devices, and the increasing need for compliance with data protection regulations.
