<p><p>Elevated scrapers are earthmoving machines used in construction and mining projects. There are two main market types for elevated scrapers: motorized and towable. Motorized scrapers are self-propelled machines equipped with a powerful engine capable of moving the equipment and carrying heavy loads. They are commonly used in large-scale construction projects where mobility and efficiency are crucial. On the other hand, towable scrapers need to be connected to a separate vehicle, such as a tractor, for transportation. They are often utilized in smaller construction sites or agricultural applications where mobility may not be a primary concern.</p></p>
