<p><p>The Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP) market consists of two types: Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC) and Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP). COC is a lightweight material with high transparency, low protein absorption, and excellent chemical resistance, making it widely used in medical packaging, optics, and pharmaceutical applications. On the other hand, COP is a homopolymer of cyclic olefins, offering exceptional heat resistance, low water absorption, and superior dimensional stability. COP finds applications in lenses, optical sensors, electronic components, and bioassay plates. Both types contribute to the growing COP market in various industries.</p></p>
