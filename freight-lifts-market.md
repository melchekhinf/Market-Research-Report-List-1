<p><p>The freight lifts market consists of two principal types: hydraulic freight lifts and electric freight lifts. Hydraulic freight lifts are powered by fluid pressure, utilizing hydraulic cylinders to raise and lower loads. They are considered more suitable for low-rise buildings. On the other hand, electric freight lifts employ an electric motor to operate a system of pulleys and counterweights. These lifts are more efficient and can be installed in buildings of various heights. Both types cater to the transportation of goods and materials within buildings.</p></p>
