<p><p>The refrigerant gas market is divided into various types, including HCFC-22, R134a, and R404a. HCFC-22, also known as chlorodifluoromethane, is used as a refrigerant in air conditioning and commercial refrigeration systems. R134a, or 1,1,1,2-tetrafluoroethane, is a commonly used refrigerant in automotive air conditioning systems. It is non-toxic and non-flammable, making it a safer alternative. R404a, composed of difluoromethane (R-32), pentafluoroethane (R-125), and 1,1,1,2-tetrafluoroethane (R-134a), is widely used in commercial refrigeration and air conditioning applications. Each type of refrigerant gas serves specific purposes in various cooling systems.</p></p>
