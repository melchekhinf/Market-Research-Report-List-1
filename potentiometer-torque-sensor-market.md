Swivel types utilize a ball-and-socket joint to allow movement in multiple directions, providing flexibility and accurate torque measurement. Gear types use gears to convert torque into rotational movement, enabling precise and reliable torque measurement. Torsion bar types use a bar that twists under a torque load, translating the torque into mechanical strain and providing accurate measurement. These different types cater to various applications and industries where torque measurement is crucial.</p></p>
