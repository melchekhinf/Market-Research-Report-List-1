<p><ul><li>Casting Molding</li><li>Press Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen quartz countertops market includes different types such as casting molding and press molding. Casting molding involves the process of pouring liquid quartz material into a mold and then allowing it to solidify and cool. This method enables the production of unique designs and shapes. On the other hand, press molding involves using a combination of high pressure and heat to compress quartz particles together, resulting in a solid and durable countertop with a consistent pattern. Both methods offer different benefits for consumers looking to enhance their kitchen with stylish and durable quartz countertops.</p></p>
