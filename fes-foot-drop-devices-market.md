<p><p>The FES Foot Drop Devices market consists of different types based on the technology used. Plantar pressure sensor-based devices measure the pressure under the foot and stimulate the muscles accordingly. Tilt sensor-based devices detect changes in body position and trigger muscle stimulation. Gyroscope and acceleration sensor-based devices use motion sensors to detect movement and provide appropriate muscle stimulation. All these devices aim to assist individuals with foot drop in regaining normal walking by stimulating the muscles involved in foot movement.</p></p>
