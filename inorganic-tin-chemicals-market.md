Tin compounds are used in the manufacturing of automotive parts, particularly in the production of advanced coatings and paints. The booming automotive industry, especially in emerging economies, is expected to boost the demand for inorganic tin chemicals.</p><p>Moreover, the packaging industry is witnessing a surge in demand for inorganic tin chemicals.
