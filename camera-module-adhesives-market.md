<p><p>The Camera Module Adhesives Market can be categorized into two types: UV curable type and non-UV curable type. UV curable adhesives are those that require ultraviolet light to initiate the curing process, making them ideal for quick and precise bonding applications. On the other hand, non-UV curable adhesives do not require UV light for curing and offer a wider range of curing mechanisms, such as heat, moisture, or chemical reactions. Both types have their own advantages and are commonly used in camera module assembly for their respective properties.</p></p>
