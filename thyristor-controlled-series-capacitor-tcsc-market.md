<p><p>Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) is a power electronics device used to enhance the performance of power systems by providing control over reactive power flow. It is a combination of a capacitor and a thyristor-controlled reactor (TCR) connected in series. TCSC offers dynamic control over the impedance of a transmission line, thereby improving the power transfer capacity and stability of the grid.</p><p>The TCSC market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blocking Mode</li><li>Bypass Mode</li><li>Capacitive Boost Mode (Capacitive Vernier Mode)</li><li>Inductive Boost Mode (Inductive Vernier Mode)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) is used in electrical power transmission systems to control voltage and increase power transfer capability. In the blocking mode, TCSC blocks the reactive power, reducing voltage fluctuations. In bypass mode, TCSC bypasses reactive power to stabilize the system. Capacitive and inductive boost modes are used to increase or decrease reactive power to balance the system. Capacitive boost mode adds extra capacitive reactance, while inductive boost mode adds extra inductive reactance, improving system stability and power flow.</p></p>
