<p><p>Below-grade waterproofing is a market that offers different types of waterproofing solutions for structures that are partially or entirely underground. These solutions include bitumen, bentonite, and rubberized asphalt. </p><p>Bitumen waterproofing involves the application of asphalt-based coatings or membranes to protect against water infiltration. Bentonite waterproofing utilizes sodium bentonite clay to create a barrier that swells upon contact with water, effectively sealing off any potential leaks. Rubberized asphalt waterproofing uses a mixture of asphalt and rubber to provide a flexible and durable layer of protection against water penetration.</p></p>
