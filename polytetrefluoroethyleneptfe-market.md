<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is a versatile synthetic polymer used in various industries. The PTFE market is divided into four types. Granular PTFE is in powder form, ideal for compression molding and extrusion processes. Fine PTFE has a lower average particle size, making it suitable for applications like coatings and lubricants. Aqueous Dispersion PTFE is a water-based liquid, widely used in coatings and impregnation processes due to its easy application. Micronized PTFE has extremely small particle size, enhancing properties like wear resistance and reducing friction, making it commonly used in lubricants and additives.</p></p>
