<p><p>Application Management Services (AMS) refer to the outsourcing of tasks related to managing and maintaining software applications. The AMS market can be divided into two types based on the nature of the services provided. Discrete AMS involves a separate service provider offering application management services independently from the application development process. Embedded AMS, on the other hand, involves a service provider integrated within the application development process, responsible for the ongoing management and support of the developed applications. Both types cater to the need for efficient application support and maintenance, but differ in terms of their integration within the development process.</p></p>
