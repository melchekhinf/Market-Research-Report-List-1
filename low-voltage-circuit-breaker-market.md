<p><p>The low-voltage circuit breaker market is divided into three main types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are compact and designed for residential use, protecting electrical circuits and appliances from overloads and short circuits. MCCBs are larger and suitable for industrial applications, providing protection for higher current levels. ACBs are used for higher voltage requirements, offering advanced protection and control features for large-scale electrical systems. These circuit breakers serve different purposes but all contribute to electrical safety in various settings.</p></p>
