The market for EC windows can be categorized into two types: Polymer Laminate EC and All-solid-state EC. Polymer Laminate EC windows consist of a polymer layer sandwiched between two conductive electrodes, while All-solid-state EC windows utilize inorganic materials as the active layer. Both types offer benefits such as energy efficiency, glare reduction, and privacy control, making them sought-after options in the architectural and automotive sectors.</p></p>
