<p><p>The Aromatic Extract market includes the Direct Chlorination (DC) process and Hydrochlorination (HC) process types. In the Direct Chlorination process, aromatic compounds are synthesized by chlorinating hydrocarbons directly. This method offers advantages of being simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, the Hydrochlorination process involves the addition of hydrogen chloride to hydrocarbons, resulting in the formation of aromatic extracts. This process is preferred due to its ability to produce higher yields and purer aromatic compounds. Both processes play significant roles in the Aromatic Extract market.</p></p>
