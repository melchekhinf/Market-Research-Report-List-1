<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medicine checkweigher market consists of two types: semi-automatic and fully automatic checkweighers. Semi-automatic checkweighers require some manual intervention during the weighing process, such as placement and removal of the medicine item. They are typically more affordable and suitable for smaller-scale operations. On the other hand, fully automatic checkweighers are capable of handling the entire weighing process automatically, without any manual involvement. They are more expensive but offer higher efficiency and throughput, making them ideal for larger medicine production facilities.</p></p>
