<p><p>Polysomnography is a diagnostic tool used to monitor and record multiple physiological activities during sleep. Different types of polysomnographs are available with varying channel numbers. A 10-channel polysomnograph can monitor essential signals like airflow, respiratory effort, and oxygen levels. A 16-channel device adds channels for leg movements and electroencephalogram (EEG) measurements. A 24-channel system includes additional channels for monitoring eye movements and heart rate. The 32-channel polysomnograph provides the most comprehensive sleep analysis by including more channels for additional brain wave recordings and muscle activity tracking.</p></p>
