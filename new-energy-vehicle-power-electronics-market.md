On-Board Charger converts alternating current (AC) from an external source into direct current (DC) to charge the vehicle's battery. The Inverter converts DC power from the battery into AC power to run the electric motor. Vehicle Control Unit (VCU) or Hybrid Control Unit (HCU) manages overall vehicle operations. Pedestrian Detection System helps prevent accidents by detecting pedestrians near the vehicle.
