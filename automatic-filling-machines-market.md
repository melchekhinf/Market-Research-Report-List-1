<p><p>Automatic filling machines market can be categorized into two types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic machines are designed to carry out the entire filling process without the need for human intervention, making them highly efficient and reducing the chances of errors. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of human operation, such as placing containers or initiating the filling process, but still minimize the manual effort involved. Both types offer benefits in terms of speed, accuracy, and productivity, catering to various needs in different industries.</p></p>
