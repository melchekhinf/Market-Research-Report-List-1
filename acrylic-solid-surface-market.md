<p><ul><li>Casting Molding Solid Surface</li><li>Extrusion Molding Solid Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acrylic Solid Surface market comprises two main types: Casting Molding Solid Surface and Extrusion Molding Solid Surface. Casting Molding Solid Surface involves pouring the liquid acrylic material into molds, which are then heated and cooled to form solid sheets. This method allows for the creation of unique designs and shapes. Extrusion Molding Solid Surface, on the other hand, involves pushing the acrylic material through a die to create continuous sheets with consistent thickness. This method is more suitable for mass production and uniform patterns.</p></p>
