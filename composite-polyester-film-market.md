<p><p>Composite polyester film is a type of film made from multiple layers of polyester material. It is used in various industries like packaging, electronics, and automotive. Biaxial tension type refers to composite polyester film that is stretched in both the machine direction (MD) and the transverse direction (TD) during the manufacturing process. This results in a film with high tensile strength and good dimensional stability. On the other hand, uniaxial tension type refers to composite polyester film that is stretched only in either the MD or the TD, leading to different properties and applications.</p></p>
