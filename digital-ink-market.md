<p><p>The digital ink market can be categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Solvent-based inks are made using organic solvents and are known for their long-lasting durability. Water-based inks are made using water as a carrier and are environmentally friendly. UV-cured inks dry quickly when exposed to ultraviolet light and provide high-quality prints. Other types of digital inks include latex-based inks, which offer high flexibility and durability, and dye-sublimation inks, which are commonly used for printing onto fabrics and textiles. Each type of ink has its unique advantages and applications in the digital printing industry.</p></p>
