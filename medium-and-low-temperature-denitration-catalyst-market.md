<p><p>Medium and Low Temperature Denitration Catalyst Market refers to the market for catalysts used to remove nitrogen oxides (NOx) from industrial emissions at medium and low temperatures. This market can be categorized into three types: honeycomb, plate, and corrugated. Honeycomb catalysts have a structured design with many small channels, allowing for high surface area and efficiency in NOx removal. Plate catalysts consist of flat plates stacked together, providing a larger contact surface and efficient performance. Corrugated catalysts have a wrinkled surface, maximizing contact area and ensuring effective NOx reduction.</p></p>
