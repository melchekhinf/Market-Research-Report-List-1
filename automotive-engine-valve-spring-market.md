<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Valve Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isometric Spring</li><li>Non-isometric Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine valve spring market is divided into two types - isometric spring and non-isometric spring. An isometric spring refers to a spring that possesses a constant wire diameter throughout its length, providing consistent force when compressed or expanded. On the other hand, a non-isometric spring includes varying wire diameter and coil spacing, resulting in different forces at different stages of compression or expansion. Both types are crucial in automotive engines as they ensure proper valve operation and contribute to optimum engine performance.</p></p>
