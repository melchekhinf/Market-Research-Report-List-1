<p><p>Disposable filtering facepiece respirators (FFRs) are used to minimize exposure to hazardous airborne particles, including viruses and bacteria. The market for FFRs can be categorized into two types: valved and unvalved. Valved FFRs are equipped with a one-way valve, allowing easy exhalation and reducing heat buildup, making them more comfortable to wear for extended periods. Unvalved FFRs, on the other hand, lack this valve and provide filtration in both directions, making them suitable for protecting both the wearer and others in close proximity. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring respiratory protection.</p></p>
