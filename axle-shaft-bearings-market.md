<p><p>Axle shaft bearings are categorized into two market types - Type I and Type II. Type I axle shaft bearings refer to those that are commonly used in light-duty vehicles, such as passenger cars and smaller trucks. These bearings are designed to handle lower loads and have dimensions suitable for these vehicle types. On the other hand, Type II axle shaft bearings are made for heavy-duty vehicles, like commercial trucks and large SUVs. They are more robust, capable of withstanding higher loads, and have larger dimensions to accommodate the needs of such vehicles.</p></p>
