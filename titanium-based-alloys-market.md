<p><p>Titanium-based alloys are widely used in various industries due to their exceptional strength, lightness, and resistance to corrosion. These alloys can be classified into three main types: Alpha Titanium Alloy, Beta Titanium Alloy, and Alpha + Beta Titanium Alloy. </p><p>Alpha Titanium Alloy consists primarily of alpha phase titanium, which has low strength but high ductility. Beta Titanium Alloy contains a large amount of beta phase titanium, which has high strength but low ductility. Alpha + Beta Titanium Alloy combines both alpha and beta phase titanium, offering a balance of strength and ductility. These different types cater to the specific requirements of different applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and medical.</p></p>
