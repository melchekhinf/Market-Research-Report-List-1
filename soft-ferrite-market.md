<p><p>The soft ferrite market can be divided into four main types: Mn-Zn, Cu-Zn, Ni-Zn, and Others. Mn-Zn ferrites are widely used in applications such as power transformers, inductors, and chokes due to their high magnetic permeability and low cost. Cu-Zn ferrites are primarily utilized in telecommunication devices and high-frequency power supplies. Ni-Zn ferrites have excellent high-frequency characteristics, making them suitable for applications like EMI filters and isolators. Lastly, the category of "Others" includes specialty ferrites with unique properties like lithium ferrite used in mobile phones and barium ferrite used in magnetic tapes.</p></p>
