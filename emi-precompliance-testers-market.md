<p><strong>The EMI Precompliance Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiated and Conducted EMI Testing</li><li>Sensitivity and Immunity EMI Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMI precompliance testers market includes two main types of testing: radiated and conducted EMI testing. Radiated EMI testing focuses on measuring the electromagnetic emissions radiated by a device, while conducted EMI testing measures the electromagnetic emissions conducted through cables and other connections. Additionally, there is sensitivity testing which evaluates the device's susceptibility to external electromagnetic disturbances and immunity testing that assesses its resilience to such disturbances. These testers are used to ensure compliance with EMI standards and to identify potential issues before final certification testing.</p></p>
