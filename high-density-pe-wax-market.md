<p><p>The High Density PE Wax market can be categorized into three types based on their production methods. Polymerization High Density PE Wax is produced by polymerizing ethylene monomers, resulting in a wax with high molecular weight and excellent hardness. Modification High Density PE Wax is made by modifying low molecular weight polyethylene, enhancing its properties like crystallinity and melting point. Thermal Cracking High Density PE Wax is obtained by thermally cracking high molecular weight polyethylene, resulting in a low molecular weight wax with good lubricating properties.</p></p>
