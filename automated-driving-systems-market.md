<p><p>Automated Driving Systems (ADS) can be categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to systems that are designed for certain specific driving tasks and operate within limited conditions or environments. They do not offer the complete automation of a vehicle's functions and require human intervention in certain situations. On the other hand, Type II ADS are more advanced and provide full automation for a wider range of driving tasks and environments.
