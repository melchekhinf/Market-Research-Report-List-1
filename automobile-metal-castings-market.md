<p><p>The automobile metal castings market is divided into several types, including steel casting, magnesium castings, aluminium casting, zinc casting, and others. Magnesium castings are made by pouring molten magnesium into a mold to create lightweight components. Aluminium casting follows a similar procedure but uses aluminum instead. Zinc casting is a method of producing parts with high precision and close tolerances. The "others" category includes various materials like iron and bronze used for casting automobile parts.</p></p>
