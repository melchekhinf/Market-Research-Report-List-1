<p><p>The IGBT Static Synchronous Compensator (STATCOM) market can be categorized into two types: Low Voltage STATCOM and High Voltage STATCOM. The Low Voltage STATCOM refers to the devices designed for systems operating at low voltage levels, typically below 69 kV. They are used to provide reactive power compensation and voltage regulation in distribution networks. On the other hand, High Voltage STATCOM is used for systems operating at higher voltage levels, generally above 69 kV. They also offer reactive power support and voltage stabilization, but cater to larger transmission networks.</p></p>
