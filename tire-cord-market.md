<p><p>The tire cord market consists of three main types: steel tire cord, polyester tire cord, and nylon tire cord. Steel tire cord is made from high-strength steel wires and is primarily used in heavy-duty tires, such as those for trucks and buses. Polyester tire cord is composed of synthetic polyester fibers and is commonly used in passenger car and light truck tires due to its excellent dimensional stability and low heat build-up. Nylon tire cord is made from nylon fibers and provides high strength and durability, making it suitable for all types of tires.</p></p>
