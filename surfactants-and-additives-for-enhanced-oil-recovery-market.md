<p><p>Surfactants and additives play a crucial role in the enhanced oil recovery (EOR) market. Surfactants are surface-active substances that reduce the interfacial tension between oil and water, allowing for improved oil displacement and recovery. They come in two types: cationic and anionic. Additives, on the other hand, are chemicals added to enhance the performance of surfactants or modify the reservoir conditions. Different types of additives include polymer and alkali additives, which improve the effectiveness of surfactants in increasing oil recovery. Together, surfactants and additives help maximize oil production in EOR operations.</p></p>
