<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The elastomeric rubber sheeting market comprises various types, including NBR-based, EPDM-based, Chloroprene-based, and others. NBR-based rubber sheets are made from nitrile butadiene rubber and exhibit excellent oil resistance properties. EPDM-based sheets are manufactured using Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer rubber and are known for their resistance to UV rays and harsh weather conditions. Chloroprene-based sheets consist of polychloroprene rubber and are highly resistant to chemicals and extreme temperatures. Lastly, the "others" category includes rubber sheets made from different elastomers, such as natural rubber, silicone rubber, and fluorocarbon rubber.</p></p>
