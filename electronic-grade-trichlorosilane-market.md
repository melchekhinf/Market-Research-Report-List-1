<p><p>The Electronic Grade Trichlorosilane (TCS) market consists of two main types of processes: Direct Chlorination (DC) and Hydrochlorination (HC). In the DC process, high-purity silicon metal reacts with chlorine gas directly, resulting in the production of TCS. On the other hand, the HC process involves the reaction of silicon tetrachloride (STC) with hydrogen gas, leading to the formation of TCS. Both processes are used in the production of TCS, which serves as a crucial raw material in the electronics industry for the production of silicon wafers and semiconductors.</p></p>
