<p><p>The market for automotive lateral position lighting fixtures can be classified into three types: halogen, xenon, and LED. Halogen lighting fixtures use a tungsten filament and a halogen gas to produce light. They are the most common and cost-effective option. Xenon lighting fixtures employ xenon gas to produce a brighter and whiter light, providing better visibility. LED lighting fixtures use light-emitting diodes to produce light, offering high energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and flexibility in design. These three types cater to different consumer preferences and lighting needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
