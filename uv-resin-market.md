<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The UV Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne UV Resins</li><li>100% Solids UV Resins</li><li>Waterborne UV Resins</li><li>Powder UV Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV resin market can be categorized into different types based on their solvents and composition. Solventborne UV resins are made with a solvent mixed with the resin, which evaporates during the curing process. 100% Solids UV resins do not contain any solvents and cure entirely through a chemical reaction. Waterborne UV resins use water as a solvent and are eco-friendly. Powder UV resins are in a powdered form and are typically used in powder coating applications. These different types cater to different needs and preferences in the UV resin market.</p></p>
