<p><p>New Energy Vehicles (NEVs) are a growing segment in the automotive industry, aimed at reducing dependence on fossil fuels and minimizing environmental impact. There are three main types of NEVs in the market: Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs), Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), and Electric Vehicles (EVs). HEVs utilize both an internal combustion engine and an electric motor to improve efficiency. PHEVs have a larger battery capacity and can be charged externally, providing a limited electric-only range. EVs solely rely on electricity for propulsion, offering zero-emissions driving. These market types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the transition towards sustainable transportation.</p></p>
