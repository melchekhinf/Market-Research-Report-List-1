<p><p>The market for medical disposable protective clothing can be categorized into two main types: polyethylene and polypropylene. Polyethylene protective clothing is made from a lightweight synthetic material that offers excellent resistance to liquids and chemicals. It is commonly used in medical settings where there is a higher risk of liquid exposure. On the other hand, polypropylene protective clothing is more durable and provides greater protection against particulate matter and biological hazards, making it suitable for applications such as laboratories and cleanrooms. Both types are crucial in ensuring the safety and hygiene of healthcare professionals.</p></p>
