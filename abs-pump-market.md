<p><p>The ABS pump market consists of two main types: electronic ABS pumps and mechanical ABS pumps. Electronic ABS pumps utilize electronic systems to control the braking process, allowing for quicker and more precise adjustments to braking forces. They are commonly found in modern vehicles. On the other hand, mechanical ABS pumps rely on mechanical components to regulate the braking system. These pumps are not as advanced as electronic ones and are usually used in older vehicles. Both types serve the purpose of enhancing braking performance and safety.</p></p>
