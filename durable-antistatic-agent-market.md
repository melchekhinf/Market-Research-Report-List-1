Cationic Antistatic Agents are positively charged and are commonly used in synthetic fibers. Anionic Antistatic Agents are negatively charged and are primarily used in coatings and films. Nonionic Antistatic Agents have no charge and are suitable for a wide range of applications. These types of agents help reduce static charges and improve the performance of materials in various industries such as textiles, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
