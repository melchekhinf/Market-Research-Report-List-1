The ability of synthetic fiber gaskets to withstand high temperatures, pressure, and chemical exposure makes them ideal for sealing applications in these industries.</p><p>Moreover, the growing focus on reducing emissions and improving fuel efficiency in the automotive industry is further boosting the demand for synthetic fiber gaskets. These gaskets help in sealing the engine components, preventing leakages and ensuring optimal performance.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a rising demand for gaskets made from advanced synthetic fibers such as carbon fibers.
