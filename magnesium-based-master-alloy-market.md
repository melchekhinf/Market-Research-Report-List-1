<p><p>A magnesium-based master alloy is an alloy consisting of magnesium as the matrix metal and one or more other elements or alloying agents that are dissolved in the matrix. These alloys are primarily used as grain refiners and modifiers in the manufacturing of magnesium alloys. They are added in small amounts to improve the mechanical and physical properties of magnesium, such as its strength, hardness, and corrosion resistance.</p><p>The magnesium-based master alloy market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for lightweight materials in various industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics. Magnesium alloys offer excellent strength-to-weight ratio, which makes them suitable for lightweight applications. The growing emphasis on fuel efficiency and reduced carbon emissions in the automotive industry is also driving the demand for magnesium-based master alloys.</p><p>In addition, the expanding electronics industry, particularly the demand for lightweight and durable electronic devices, is bolstering the market growth. Magnesium-based master alloys are used in the production of electronic housings and connectors due to their superior thermal conductivity and electrical resistivity.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing investment in research and development activities for the development of advanced magnesium alloys is further fueling the market growth. These efforts are expected to drive the demand for magnesium-based master alloys in the forecast period.</p><p>Overall, the magnesium-based master alloy market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
