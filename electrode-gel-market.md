<p><strong>The Electrode Gel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halide-Containing Gel</li><li>Non-Halide Gel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrode gel is available in different types to cater to various needs. Halide-containing gel refers to a gel that contains halide ions, such as chloride or bromide, which enhance the conductivity of the gel for better electrode-skin contact. On the other hand, non-halide gel is a type of gel that does not contain halide ions but instead utilizes alternative conductive substances like salt-free solutions or organic materials. Both gel types are used in medical diagnostics, sports therapy, and other applications to improve the accuracy and effectiveness of electrodes.</p></p>
