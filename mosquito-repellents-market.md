<p><ul><li>Coils</li><li>Vaporizer</li><li>Mats</li><li>Aerosols</li><li>Creams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mosquito repellents are available in various forms, including coils, vaporizers, mats, aerosols, and creams. Coils are commonly used in tropical regions and are burned to emit smoke that repels mosquitoes. Vaporizers work by heating a liquid repellent to release a vapor in the air. Mats release a chemical into the atmosphere when heated, acting as a mosquito deterrent. Aerosols are sprayed directly onto the skin or in the air to repel mosquitoes. Creams are applied topically and create a protective barrier on the skin to keep mosquitoes away. These different types cater to individuals' preferences and varying needs for protection against mosquitoes.</p></p>
