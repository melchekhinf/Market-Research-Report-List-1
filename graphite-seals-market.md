<p><strong>The Graphite Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible</li><li>Inflexible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite seals are widely used for various industrial applications due to their high temperature resistance and low friction properties. In the market, there are two main types of graphite seals: flexible and inflexible. </p><p>Flexible graphite seals are made from expanded graphite sheets that can be easily compressed and molded into different shapes, allowing for tight sealing in complex geometries. These seals are often used in applications where there is a need for flexibility, such as in automotive engines or pumps, where the seal needs to accommodate movement.</p><p>On the other hand, inflexible graphite seals are made from pure graphite that is rigid and cannot be easily compressed or molded. These seals are typically used in applications where there is no need for flexibility but a high level of thermal stability is required, such as in high-temperature furnaces or aerospace applications.</p><p>In summary, the choice between flexible and inflexible graphite seals depends on the specific requirements of the application, with flexible seals providing adaptability and inflexible seals offering enhanced thermal stability.</p></p>
