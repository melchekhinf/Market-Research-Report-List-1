<p><p>The 3C product coating market consists of two major types of coatings: UV curing coating and thermal curing coating. UV curing coatings utilize ultraviolet light to rapidly cure and form a protective layer on the surface of 3C products. This type of coating offers high efficiency and does not emit harmful substances during the curing process. On the other hand, thermal curing coatings rely on heat to cure and create a durable coating. These coatings are known for their exceptional adhesion and resistance to high temperatures.</p></p>
