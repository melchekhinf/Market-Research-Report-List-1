<p><p>The rapeseed protein market consists of two main types: hydrolyzed rapeseed protein and rapeseed protein isolate. Hydrolyzed rapeseed protein is produced by breaking down the protein into smaller fragments, resulting in enhanced digestibility and absorption. This type of protein is commonly used in sports nutrition products and dietary supplements. On the other hand, rapeseed protein isolate is obtained by isolating pure protein from rapeseed, removing other components and impurities. It is used in various food and beverage applications, such as bakery products, meat analogs, and dairy alternatives.</p></p>
