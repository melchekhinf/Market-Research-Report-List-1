<p><p>The mineral wool acoustic panels market is categorized into two main types: Glass Fiber Wool and Mineral Wool. Glass Fiber Wool panels are manufactured using fine glass fibers that are bonded together to form a dense structure. These panels are known for their high sound absorption properties and are often used in commercial and industrial buildings. On the other hand, Mineral Wool panels are made from natural minerals such as basalt or slag. They offer excellent thermal insulation and fire resistance, making them suitable for both residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
