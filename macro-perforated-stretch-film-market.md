<p><p>The Macro Perforated Stretch Film market consists of two main types: Hand Film and Machine Film. Hand Film refers to the film that is manually applied by hand. It is commonly used in small-scale applications or when the wrapping needs to be done quickly and easily. On the other hand, Machine Film is designed to be used with automated or semi-automated wrapping machines. It is typically used in large-scale applications or when higher levels of efficiency and consistency are required. Both types of films provide perforations that enhance breathability and ventilation for the packaged goods.</p></p>
