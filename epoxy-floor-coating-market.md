<p><p>The epoxy floor coating market can be segmented into two main types: solvent epoxy floor coating and non-solvent epoxy floor coating. Solvent epoxy floor coatings contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) that evaporate during the curing process. In contrast, non-solvent epoxy floor coatings do not contain VOCs, making them environmentally friendly. Solvent epoxy floor coatings are more commonly used in industrial settings due to their high durability, while non-solvent epoxy floor coatings are preferred in residential and commercial applications for their low odor and ease of application.</p></p>
