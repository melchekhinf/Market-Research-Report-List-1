<p><p>A Laboratory Information System (LIS) is a software-based system that manages and stores lab test results and related data. There are two main types of LIS markets: On-premises LIS and Cloud-Based LIS. On-premises LIS refers to software installed and maintained on local servers within the lab premises. It enables complete control over data security and customization. In contrast, Cloud-Based LIS is a web-based system hosted on remote servers, accessible over the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as the system maintenance is outsourced.</p></p>
