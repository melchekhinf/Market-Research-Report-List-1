<p><p>The Automotive Power Liftgate market consists of two types: Conventional Power Liftgate and Hands-free Power Liftgate. Conventional Power Liftgate refers to the traditional liftgate system operated manually or with the help of buttons inside the vehicle. On the other hand, Hands-free Power Liftgate allows users to open or close the liftgate without physically touching it. This type of liftgate utilizes sensors or foot gestures to detect the user's presence and perform the desired action, offering convenience and ease of use for consumers.</p></p>
