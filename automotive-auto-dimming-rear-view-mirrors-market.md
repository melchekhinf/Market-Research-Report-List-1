<p><p>The automotive auto dimming rear view mirrors market can be divided into two types: outer auto dimming mirrors and inside auto dimming mirrors. Outer auto dimming mirrors are located on the exterior of the vehicle and automatically dim the reflected light from the headlights of trailing vehicles, reducing glare for the driver. Inside auto dimming mirrors are mounted on the inside of the vehicle and work similarly, dimming the reflected light from headlights of vehicles approaching from the rear. Both types enhance driving safety by improving visibility and reducing distractions caused by glare.</p></p>
