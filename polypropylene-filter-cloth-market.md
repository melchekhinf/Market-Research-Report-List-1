<p><p>The Polypropylene Filter Cloth Market encompasses two types: Polypropylene Staple Fiber Cloth and Polypropylene Long Fiber Filter Cloth. Polypropylene Staple Fiber Cloth is composed of short lengths of polypropylene fibers, offering high filtration efficiency while maintaining strong mechanical properties. On the other hand, Polypropylene Long Fiber Filter Cloth consists of long-length polypropylene fibers, providing excellent cake release and enhanced permeability. Both types are widely used in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and wastewater treatment for their efficient filtration capabilities and durability.</p></p>
