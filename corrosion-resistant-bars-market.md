<p><p>Corrosion-resistant bars are used in the construction industry to prevent rust and corrosion in reinforced concrete structures. There are different types of corrosion-resistant bars available in the market. Epoxy-coated reinforcing steel bars have a protective epoxy coating, while galvanized reinforcing steel bars are coated with a layer of zinc. Low carbon, chromium reinforcing steel bars contain chromium for increased corrosion resistance. Dual-coated reinforcing steel bars have both zinc and epoxy coatings. Stainless steel bars are made from corrosion-resistant stainless steel materials.</p></p>
