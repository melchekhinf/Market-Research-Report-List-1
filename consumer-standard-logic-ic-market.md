<p><strong>The Consumer Standard Logic IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TTL IC (Transistor-transistor Logic)</li><li>CMOS Logic IC (Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor)</li><li>BiCMOS Logic IC (Bipolar CMOS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Consumer standard logic ICs are electronic devices that perform logic operations in consumer electronics products. There are different types of logic ICs in the market. </p><p>TTL ICs, or Transistor-transistor Logic ICs, use bipolar transistors for operation and are known for their high-speed performance and noise immunity. </p><p>CMOS Logic ICs, or Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor ICs, use both NMOS and PMOS transistors to reduce power consumption and provide improved noise immunity. </p><p>BiCMOS Logic ICs, or Bipolar CMOS ICs, combine the advantages of both TTL and CMOS technologies, providing higher performance and lower power consumption compared to TTL ICs.</p></p>
