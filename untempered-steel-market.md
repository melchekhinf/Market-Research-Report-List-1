There are three types of untempered steel market types: ferrite-pearlite steel, bainite steel, and martensitic steel. </p><p>Ferrite-pearlite steel is a combination of ferrite, a soft and ductile phase, and pearlite, a harder and stronger phase. </p><p>Bainite steel is characterized by a fine microstructure, resulting in good toughness and strength. </p><p>Martensitic steel has a higher carbon content, increasing its hardness and wear resistance. </p><p>Each type of untempered steel offers unique properties and is used in various applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
