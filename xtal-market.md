<p><p>Xtal, or crystal oscillators, are devices used in electronic circuits to generate precise clock signals. These are available in different market types such as DIP Type and SMD Type. DIP Type crystals feature a dual in-line package, which refers to two rows of pins that can be inserted into a circuit board. On the other hand, SMD Type crystals are surface-mount devices that can be soldered directly onto circuit boards. Both types cater to different circuit designs and manufacturing requirements, providing options for engineers and manufacturers to select the most suitable type for their specific applications.</p></p>
