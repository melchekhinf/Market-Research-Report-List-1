<p><strong>The Automotive Aerodynamic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active System</li><li>Passive System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive aerodynamic system market is divided into two main types: active systems and passive systems. Active systems refer to advanced technologies that actively optimize the vehicle's aerodynamic performance, such as active grille shutters or adjustable spoilers. These systems actively respond to varying driving conditions or vehicle speeds to enhance fuel efficiency and reduce drag. On the other hand, passive systems consist of features incorporated into the vehicle's design that improve aerodynamics without requiring any external input or adjustment. Examples include streamlined body shapes or underbody panels that reduce airflow turbulence.</p></p>
