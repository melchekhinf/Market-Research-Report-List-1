<p><p>The biologic hernia repair mesh market consists of two main types of mesh: partially absorbable and fully absorbable. Partially absorbable hernia repair mesh is made from a combination of biologic materials and synthetic materials. It provides immediate support to the hernia site and gradually degrades over time, allowing the patient's own tissue to grow and replace the mesh. Fully absorbable hernia repair mesh, on the other hand, is made entirely from biologic materials and is designed to completely degrade and be replaced by natural tissue within a specified period of time.</p></p>
