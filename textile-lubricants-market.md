<p><p>The textile lubricants market consists of various types of lubricants used in different stages of textile production. Spin Finish lubricants are applied to fibers during spinning to reduce friction and enhance productivity. Coning Oils are used in the winding process to ensure smooth yarn winding and prevent breakage. Knitting Oils are applied to knitting machines to reduce friction and wear, improving the quality of knitted fabrics. Greases are used for lubricating textile machinery, reducing friction, and extending the machines' lifespan. These lubricants play a crucial role in optimizing textile production processes.</p></p>
