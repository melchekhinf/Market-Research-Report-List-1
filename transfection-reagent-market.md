<p><strong>The Transfection Reagent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biochemical Transfection</li><li>Physical Transfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transfection reagents are used to introduce nucleic acids into cells for various research purposes. The biochemical transfection market involves the use of lipid-based or polymer-based reagents that form complexes with nucleic acids and deliver them into cells. This method is widely used due to its simplicity and efficiency. On the other hand, physical transfection involves techniques such as electroporation or microinjection, which physically disrupt cell membrane to allow nucleic acid entry. This method is preferred for specific applications requiring higher transfection efficiency or for challenging cell types.</p></p>
