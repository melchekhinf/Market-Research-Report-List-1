<p><p>The boron powder market can be classified into two types: amorphous and crystalline. Amorphous boron powder is a non-crystalline form of boron that lacks a defined crystal structure. It is often used as a doping agent in semiconductors and as a fuel for igniters due to its high energy content. On the other hand, crystalline boron powder has a well-defined crystal structure and is used in various industries such as ceramics, aerospace, and pharmaceuticals. It has excellent mechanical properties and thermal stability, making it highly desirable for these applications.</p></p>
