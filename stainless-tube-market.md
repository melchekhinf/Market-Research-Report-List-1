<p><strong>The Stainless Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Welded</li><li>Seamless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless tube market consists of two main types: welded and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are fabricated by joining the edges of a stainless steel strip, forming a continuous tube. They are cost-effective and widely used in various applications. Seamless tubes, on the other hand, are formed without any welding, resulting in a stronger and more durable tube. They are known for their high resistance to corrosion and are commonly used in industries such as automotive, oil and gas, and construction.</p></p>
