<p><p>An All-in-One (AIO) computer is a type of desktop computer that integrates all the major components, such as the monitor, central processing unit (CPU), memory, and storage, into a single unit. Factors driving the market include the increasing demand for space-saving and aesthetically pleasing computer solutions, along with the rising adoption of touchscreen devices. AIO computers are popular among both individual consumers and businesses, as they offer a simplified and versatile computing experience.</p><p>Another key driver for the market is the growth in the gaming industry. AIO computers equipped with high-end specifications and large displays are becoming popular choices among avid gamers, as they offer immersive gaming experiences without the need for separate monitors and CPUs.</p><p>Technological advancements and innovations in AIO computers continue to propel market growth.
