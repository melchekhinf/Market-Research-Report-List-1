<p><p>The circulating oils market is categorized into high viscosity and low viscosity types. High viscosity circulating oils have a thicker consistency and are commonly used in heavy-duty industrial applications, such as in machinery, turbines, and gearboxes. They provide better lubrication and protection under high temperature and pressure conditions. On the other hand, low viscosity circulating oils have a thinner consistency and are often used in automotive engines, providing efficient lubrication and reducing friction. These oils are designed to flow easily at lower temperatures, ensuring smooth operation of engines and improving fuel efficiency.</p></p>
