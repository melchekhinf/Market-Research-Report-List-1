<p><ul><li>Extrusion Grade</li><li>Injection Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FEP (fluorinated ethylene propylene) resin is available in two main market types: extrusion grade and injection grade. Extrusion grade FEP resin is specifically formulated for use in processes such as film extrusion, wire and cable coating, and tubing manufacturing. It has excellent melt strength and can be easily processed through extrusion machinery. On the other hand, injection grade FEP resin is designed for injection molding applications, where the resin is melted and injected into molds to create various products. It has a lower melt viscosity, allowing it to flow easily into complex mold cavities during the injection molding process.</p></p>
