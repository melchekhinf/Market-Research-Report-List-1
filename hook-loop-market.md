<p><p>Hook & Loop, commonly known as Velcro, is a fastening system consisting of two components: hooks and loops. The hooks are small, stiff, and rigid, while the loops are softer and more flexible. Hook & Loop is widely used in various industries and applications, including apparel, footwear, healthcare, automotive, aerospace, and packaging.</p><p>The future outlook for the Hook & Loop market appears to be promising.
