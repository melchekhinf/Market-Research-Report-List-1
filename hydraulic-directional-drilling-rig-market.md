<p><p>The Hydraulic Directional Drilling Rig market can be segregated into two types - Soft Ground and Hard Rock market. Soft Ground rigs are equipped with special tools and techniques to effectively navigate through loose terrain such as clay, sand, and gravel. They utilize bentonite or polymer-based mud to stabilize the borehole. On the other hand, Hard Rock rigs are specifically designed to tackle solid rocks and boulders. These rigs use advanced drilling technologies like diamond or tungsten carbide drill bits to break through and penetrate hard formations.</p></p>
