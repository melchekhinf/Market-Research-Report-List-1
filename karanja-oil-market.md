<p><p>Karanja oil is extracted from the seeds of the Pongamia pinnata or Karanja tree, which is native to India and some other Asian countries. It contains compounds like pongamol and karanjin, which have been found to have anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidative effects.
