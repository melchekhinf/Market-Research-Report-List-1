<p><ul><li>Bacterial Fertilizer</li><li>Actinomycetes Fertilizer</li><li>Fungi Fertilizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio fertilizer market consists of three main types: Bacterial fertilizer, Actinomycetes fertilizer, and Fungi fertilizer. Bacterial fertilizers contain beneficial bacteria called rhizobium that enhance nitrogen fixation in the soil, promoting plant growth. Actinomycetes fertilizers consist of actinomycetes, microorganisms that produce enzymes, nitrogen compounds, and growth regulators, contributing to soil health and plant development. Fungi fertilizers contain beneficial fungi that form symbiotic relationships with plant roots, aiding in nutrient absorption and disease resistance. These bio fertilizers offer sustainable and eco-friendly alternatives to chemical fertilizers, supporting soil fertility and crop productivity.</p></p>
