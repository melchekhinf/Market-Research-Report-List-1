<p><p>The Contour and Surface Measuring Machine market consists of two main types: Contact and Non-Contact machines. Contact Contour and Surface Measuring Machines utilize physical contact with the surface being measured, using probes or styluses to gather data on the shape and size of the object. On the other hand, Non-Contact Contour and Surface Measuring Machines utilize optical or laser technology to scan the object without physical contact, providing precise measurements and imaging. These machines are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for quality control and inspection purposes.</p></p>
