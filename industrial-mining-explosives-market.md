<p><p>The Industrial Mining Explosives market consists of different types of explosives used in mining operations. One such type is Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (Powder), which is a mixture of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil. Another type is ANFO, which stands for Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil, a popular explosive made by blending ammonium nitrate with diesel fuel. Lastly, there is the Emulsion Explosive market, which involves the use of a stable emulsion mixture containing oxidizers, fuels, and water. These explosives are commonly used in the mining industry to break and fragment rocks for easier extraction of minerals.</p></p>
