<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Steel Sheet Piles</li><li>Cold-formed steel sheet pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steel Sheet Piling market consists of two main types: Hot Rolled Steel Sheet Piles and Cold-formed Steel Sheet Piles. Hot Rolled Steel Sheet Piles are manufactured by heating and rolling steel into large beams with interlocking sections. They are known for their sturdiness and ability to resist high pressure. On the other hand, Cold-formed Steel Sheet Piles are produced by bending and forming steel sheets into interlocking sections. They are typically lighter and more cost-effective, making them suitable for less demanding applications.</p></p>
