<p><p>Colloid silica refers to a stable dispersion of silica particles in a liquid medium, typically water. The booming construction sector, coupled with the rising demand for sustainable infrastructure, is contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>Another key trend in the colloid silica market is its growing utilization in the coatings industry.
