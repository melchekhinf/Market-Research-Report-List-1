<p><p>The potato digger market comprises two main types: mounted potato diggers and trailed potato diggers. Mounted potato diggers are attached to tractors, allowing for efficient potato harvesting by digging and loosening the soil. These machines have high maneuverability and are suitable for small to medium-sized farms. Trailed potato diggers, on the other hand, are pulled by tractors and have larger working capacities. They are commonly used in large-scale commercial farming operations where high productivity is required. Both types cater to the needs of different farmers based on the size and intensity of their potato production.</p></p>
