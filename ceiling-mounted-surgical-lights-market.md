<p><p>Ceiling-mounted surgical lights are essential equipment in operating rooms that provide adequate illumination for precise medical procedures. These lights come in two primary types - One Arm and Two Arm. One Arm lights consist of a single, flexible arm attached to the ceiling, allowing the light source to be maneuvered in multiple directions. In contrast, Two Arm lights have two separate arms that can be adjusted independently, providing even greater flexibility and coverage. Both types enable surgeons to effectively illuminate the surgical area, enhancing visibility and ensuring successful operations.</p></p>
