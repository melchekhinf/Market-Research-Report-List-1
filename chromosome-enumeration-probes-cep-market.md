<p><p>Chromosome Enumeration Probes (CEP) are used in genetic testing to identify abnormal changes in chromosomes. There are three types of CEP market: Single Color Probes, Dual Color Probes, and Multi-color Probes. </p><p>Single Color Probes use a single fluorescent dye to label the target chromosome, making it easier to detect abnormalities. </p><p>Dual Color Probes use two different colored dyes to label the target chromosome and a reference chromosome, helping to identify anomalies based on color differences. </p><p>Multi-color Probes use multiple fluorescent dyes and can detect abnormalities in multiple chromosomes simultaneously, providing a comprehensive analysis of chromosomal changes.</p></p>
