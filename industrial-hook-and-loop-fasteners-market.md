<p><p>Industrial hook and loop fasteners are a type of fastening system commonly used in various industries for securing and fastening purposes. When pressed together, the hooks and loops interlock, providing a strong and secure fastening.</p><p>The industrial hook and loop fasteners market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period.
