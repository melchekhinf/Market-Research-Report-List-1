<p><p>Steel pipe coatings market includes various types such as epoxy, fusion bond epoxy, cement-mortar, polyurethane, bituminous asphalt, and others. Epoxy coatings provide high chemical resistance and adhesion to the pipe's surface. Fusion bond epoxy coatings are thermosetting coatings that offer superior corrosion protection. Cement-mortar coatings provide a thick layer of protection against corrosive elements. Polyurethane coatings offer excellent abrasion and impact resistance. Bituminous asphalt coatings provide excellent waterproofing and corrosion resistance. Other types include coal tar coatings and tape wraps, which provide additional protective layers to the steel pipes.</p></p>
