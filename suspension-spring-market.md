<p><ul><li>Coil Spring</li><li>Air Spring</li><li>Leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suspension spring market consists of various types, namely coil springs, air springs, and leaf springs. Coil springs are helical-shaped springs made of steel that provide support and absorb shocks in vehicles. Air springs are inflatable rubber or plastic chambers that use compressed air to support the load and provide a smoother ride. Leaf springs, on the other hand, are multiple layers of curved metal strips that are bundled together to support the vehicle's weight. These different types of springs are crucial components in automotive suspension systems, ensuring optimal comfort and stability.</p></p>
