APS stands for Average Particle Size, which refers to the average diameter of the particles in the anode material. In the APS 1.5 – 3 μm market, the anode material has an average particle size ranging from 1.5 to 3 micrometers. Similarly, in the APS 3 – 5 μm market, the average particle size is between 3 and 5 micrometers. The Others market category includes anode materials with particle sizes that do not fall into the above mentioned ranges.</p></p>
