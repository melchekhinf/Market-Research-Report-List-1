<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Tire market consists of two types of tires: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires are constructed with layers of cords that run radially from bead to bead, providing flexibility and strength. They are known for their better heat dissipation and fuel efficiency, offering smoother rides and longer lifespan. Bias tires, on the other hand, have several layers of cords that crisscross at a diagonal angle, offering superior resistance to damage. These tires are known for their affordability and high load-carrying capacity.</p></p>
