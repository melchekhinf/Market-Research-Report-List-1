<p><p>The cement foam market consists of two main types: liquid and solid. Liquid cement foam is a mixture of cement, water, and foam agent that can be easily pumped or sprayed into various applications. It has a low density and is commonly used in construction and insulation. On the other hand, solid cement foam is a compact version formed by injecting foam agent into a pre-formed cement matrix. It offers higher strength and density compared to the liquid foam, making it suitable for structural and load-bearing purposes.</p></p>
