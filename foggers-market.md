<p><p>Foggers are devices used for pest control, disinfection, and odor elimination. Thermal foggers are market types that use heat to vaporize chemical solutions, creating dense fog that penetrates deep into crevices. They are commonly employed for outdoor use. Cold foggers, on the other hand, use pressure to break down liquid solutions into tiny particles, creating a mist-like fog. These foggers are suitable for both indoor and outdoor use, as they cover a wider area and are more effective against flying insects and diseases.</p></p>
