<p><ul><li>Heat Activated Cover Tapes</li><li>Pressure-Sensitive Cover Tapes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-Static Cover Tape market consists of two main types: Heat Activated Cover Tapes and Pressure-Sensitive Cover Tapes. Heat Activated Cover Tapes require a certain amount of heat to become adhesive and securely seal electronic components during packaging. On the other hand, Pressure-Sensitive Cover Tapes do not require heat; they rely on pressure to stick to the surface effectively. Both types are utilized in the electronic industry to provide protection against static electricity and ensure safe transportation and storage of sensitive components.</p></p>
