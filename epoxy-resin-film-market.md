<p><p>The epoxy resin film market can be categorized into two types: transparent and opaque. </p><p>Transparent epoxy resin films are clear and allow light to pass through, providing a high level of clarity and visibility. These films are commonly used for applications that require transparency, such as in electronic displays, windows, and protective coatings.</p><p>On the other hand, opaque epoxy resin films are not see-through and do not allow light to pass through. These films are typically used for applications where light blocking or opaqueness is desired, such as in packaging materials, labeling, and privacy films.</p></p>
