Sodium dodecyl sulfate (SDS) is used to denature the proteins and confer a negative charge to them, allowing for their movement towards the anode during electrophoresis.</p><p>The current market outlook for SDS Poly Acrylamide Gel Electrophoresis is positive and shows significant growth potential.
