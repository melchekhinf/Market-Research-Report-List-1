<p><p>The polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) market is categorized into several types. Fully hydrolyzed PVA offers high water solubility and film-forming properties, making it ideal for applications like adhesives, coatings, and textile sizing. Partially hydrolyzed PVA has intermediate solubility and film-forming capabilities, suitable for applications in emulsion polymerization, paper coatings, and packaging films. Sub-partially hydrolyzed PVA combines the benefits of fully and partially hydrolyzed PVA. Low foaming grades of PVA are used in detergents, surfactants, and oil extraction processes. Other grades of PVA serve specific purposes such as pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and construction, among others.</p></p>
