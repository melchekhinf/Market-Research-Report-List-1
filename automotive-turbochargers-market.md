<p><p>Automotive turbochargers are devices that compress air entering the engine to improve performance and efficiency. Mono Turbo refers to a single turbocharger installed in the engine, providing a boost in power. Twin Turbo, on the other hand, involves two turbochargers working together, typically in a sequential manner, to deliver increased power output. Both types enhance engine performance by supplying additional compressed air, which leads to improved fuel combustion and acceleration in vehicles. These turbocharger configurations cater to different engine requirements and performance goals.</p></p>
