<p><p>A residential lightning rod, also known as a lightning protection system, is a device designed to protect homes from the potential damage caused by lightning strikes. It consists of a metal rod usually installed at the highest point of a building, connected to a network of conductors and grounding systems. When lightning strikes, the lightning rod provides a path of least resistance for the electrical current, diverting it safely into the ground.</p><p>The current outlook of the residential lightning rod market is positive, and it is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. Factors driving this growth include increasing awareness about the need for protection against lightning strikes, growing construction activities in developing countries, and rising instances of severe weather conditions due to climate change. Meshed Conductors Lightning Protection System uses a network of interconnected conductors to disperse lightning strikes. Catenary Wire Lightning Conductor uses suspended wires to intercept lightning strikes. Streamer Emission Lightning Conductor generates upward streamers to attract lightning away from structures.
