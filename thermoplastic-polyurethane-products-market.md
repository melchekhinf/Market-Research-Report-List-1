<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) products market can be classified into three types: foam, elastomer, and others. Foam TPU products are lightweight, flexible, and possess excellent shock-absorbing properties, making them widely used in the automotive and sports industry. Elastomer TPU products, on the other hand, are highly elastic, durable, and resistant to abrasion, making them suitable for applications such as belts, hoses, and seals. The "others" category includes TPU films, coatings, adhesives, and fibers, which find applications in textiles, footwear, electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
