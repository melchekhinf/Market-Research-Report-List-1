<p><p>The ambient lighting software market offers various types of lighting solutions, including surface-mounted lights, strip lights, track lights, recessed lights, and suspended lights. Surface-mounted lights are fixed directly onto the surface and provide general illumination. Strip lights are flexible and can be attached to different surfaces to create a dynamic lighting effect. Track lights are installed on tracks, allowing for adjustable positioning and focused lighting. Recessed lights are mounted into the ceiling, creating a seamless and clean appearance. Suspended lights are hung from the ceiling, providing a stylish and modern lighting solution.</p></p>
