<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Car air conditioner filters come in two main types: single effect filters and double effect filters. Single effect filters are designed to remove larger particles such as dust, pollen, and debris from the air that enters the car's air conditioning system. They provide basic filtration and are commonly used in entry-level or budget-friendly vehicles. On the other hand, double effect filters not only filter out larger particles but also have an additional layer that can capture smaller particles like bacteria and odors. They offer enhanced air purification and are typically found in high-end or luxury cars.</p></p>
