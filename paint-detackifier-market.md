<p><strong>The Paint Detackifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint detackifier market can be categorized into two major types: powder and liquid. Powder detackifiers are dry and granular substances that are applied onto wet paint overspray to neutralize and solidify it. They are easy to handle, have a longer shelf life, and are less likely to pose environmental hazards. Liquid detackifiers, on the other hand, are liquid-based chemicals that are sprayed onto the paint overspray. They quickly react with the paint particles, causing them to clump together and settle. This type offers the advantage of being easier to apply and providing a more uniform coverage.</p></p>
