<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The magnesium alloy die casting machine market comprises two types of machines: cold chamber die casting machine and hot chamber die casting machine. In a cold chamber die casting machine, the molten magnesium alloy is ladled into the cold chamber, and then a piston injects the alloy into the mold. This type is suitable for high melting point alloys. On the other hand, a hot chamber die casting machine contains a pool of molten magnesium alloy from which it draws and injects the material into the mold directly. This type is preferred for lower melting point alloys.</p></p>
