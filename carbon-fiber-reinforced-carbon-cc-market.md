<p><ul><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition</li><li>Liquid Impregnation Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Fiber-Reinforced Carbon (C/C) Market consists of two major types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process. CVD involves depositing carbon atoms on a substrate surface using a chemical reaction, resulting in a strong and high-performance material. On the other hand, the Liquid Impregnation Process entails impregnating a carbon fiber preform with a liquid carbon precursor, followed by a heat treatment to convert it into a solid carbon matrix. Both methods contribute to the overall growth of the C/C market by providing different manufacturing options based on specific requirements.</p></p>
