<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Polyurethane solution market types can be classified into water-based and solvent-based polyurethane compounds. Water-based polyurethane compounds are made by dissolving polyurethane in water, resulting in a solution that is low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and offers environmental and health benefits. Solvent-based polyurethane compounds, on the other hand, are made by dissolving polyurethane in organic solvents, providing enhanced durability and performance properties. However, they may contain a higher level of VOCs, which can have negative environmental impacts. Both types of compounds are used in various applications, such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants.</p></p>
