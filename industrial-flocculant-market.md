On the other hand, inorganic coagulants are synthetic compounds that are widely used in various industries. They are effective in removing suspended solids and contaminants from water, making them a popular choice in water treatment processes. Both types of coagulants play a crucial role in flocculation, which is the process of clumping together fine particles for easier removal or filtration.</p></p>
