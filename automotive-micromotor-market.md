<p><strong>The Automotive Micromotor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed DC Motor</li><li>Brushless DC Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive micromotor market comprises Brushed DC Motor and Brushless DC Motor types. A Brushed DC Motor is a compact device that operates using a rotating armature and a stationary field magnet, enabling energy conversion. On the other hand, a Brushless DC Motor utilizes a permanent magnet rotor and a commutator-less stator, resulting in improved durability, efficiency, and performance. These motors find extensive application in various automotive systems, such as power windows, seat adjustments, mirrors, HVAC systems, and electronic steering, for enhancing comfort and functionality in vehicles.</p></p>
