<p><p>Pressure sensitive adhesive tapes are a type of adhesive tape that stick to surfaces when light pressure is applied. These tapes consist of a pressure-sensitive adhesive coating that allows them to adhere to various materials, including metals, plastics, glass, and paper. They offer several advantages such as ease of application, strong adhesion, and versatility, making them widely used in various industries including packaging, healthcare, automotive, construction, and electronics.</p><p>The current outlook of the pressure-sensitive adhesive tapes market is positive, with steady growth in recent years. The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for these tapes in the packaging industry, as they are extensively used for carton sealing, bundling, and labeling applications. The growth of e-commerce and online retailing has further boosted the demand for pressure-sensitive adhesive tapes in the packaging sector.</p><p>In addition to packaging, the healthcare industry is also a key consumer of pressure-sensitive adhesive tapes.
