<p><p>The automotive aluminum heat exchanger market consists of two major types: HVAC heat exchangers and powertrain heat exchangers. HVAC heat exchangers are components used in the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems of vehicles to transfer heat between the refrigerant and the outside air. Powertrain heat exchangers, on the other hand, are utilized in the engine cooling systems to regulate the temperature of coolant and prevent overheating. These heat exchangers play vital roles in maintaining optimum temperature levels in vehicles for enhanced performance and passenger comfort.</p></p>
