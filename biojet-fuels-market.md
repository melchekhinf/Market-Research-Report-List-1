<p><ul><li>HEFA-SPK</li><li>FT-SPK</li><li>ATJ-SPK</li><li>Co-processing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biojet Fuels market can be categorized into various types such as HEFA-SPK, FT-SPK, ATJ-SPK, co-processing, and others. HEFA-SPK (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids-Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) involves converting bio-based oils and fats through hydroprocessing to produce jet fuel. FT-SPK (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) uses the Fischer-Tropsch process to convert biomass or coal into jet fuel. ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) employs alcohol feedstocks to produce jet fuel. Co-processing refers to the blending of petroleum-based and bio-based feedstocks to create jet fuel. Other types encompass novel methods and emerging technologies for biojet fuel production.</p></p>
