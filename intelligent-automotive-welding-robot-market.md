<p><p>The Intelligent Automotive Welding Robot market encompasses various types of welding techniques. Arc welding involves creating an electric arc between an electrode and the metal, melting and fusing them together. Brazing welding uses a filler material with lower melting point to join two or more metal pieces. Spot welding utilizes the electric resistance to generate heat and weld small areas. Laser welding employs a focused laser beam to melt and join metals. These different types serve the automotive industry's need for efficient and precise welding processes.</p></p>
