There are two main types of contact IC cards: memory cards and microprocessor cards. Memory cards have a built-in memory chip that stores data, but their functionality is limited to basic data storage and retrieval. On the other hand, microprocessor cards have an embedded microprocessor chip, enabling them to perform more complex tasks like encryption, authentication, and running applications.</p></p>
