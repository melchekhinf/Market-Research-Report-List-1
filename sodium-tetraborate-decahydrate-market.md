<p><p>Sodium Tetraborate Decahydrate, also known as borax or sodium borate, is a white, crystalline powder that consists of boron, sodium, and oxygen. It is commonly used in industries such as glass and ceramics, detergent manufacturing, agriculture, and metallurgy.</p><p>In the glass and ceramics industry, sodium tetraborate decahydrate is used as a flux to lower the melting point of the materials, improve the flow of the molten glass or ceramic, and enhance the transparency and durability of the final product. In the detergent manufacturing industry, it is used as a water softener and pH buffer. In agriculture, it is used as a micronutrient fertilizer to provide boron to the soil, which is essential for the growth of plants. In metallurgy, it is used as a flux to remove metal oxide impurities during the melting process.</p><p>The sodium tetraborate decahydrate market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
