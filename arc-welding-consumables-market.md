<p><p>Arc welding consumables refer to the materials used during the arc welding process, which is a type of welding that uses an electric arc between an electrode and the base material to create a weld. These consumables include welding electrodes, filler metals, fluxes, and shielding gases, among others. They are essential components in the arc welding process as they help in joining metal parts together by melting the materials and creating a strong bond.</p><p>The future outlook of the arc welding consumables market is positive, with steady growth expected during the forecasted period. The increasing need for infrastructure development, especially in developing economies, is also expected to contribute to the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements in arc welding consumables, such as the development of low hydrogen electrodes and flux-cored wires, are further boosting the market growth. Flux cored wires offer higher welding speed and better mechanical properties. Submerged arc welding (SAW) wires are used for heavy industrial applications.
