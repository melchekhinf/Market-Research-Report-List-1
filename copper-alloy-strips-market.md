<p><p>The copper alloy strips market consists of three main types: bronze strip, brass strip, and cupronickel strip. Bronze strip is made by combining copper with tin, and is known for its corrosion resistance and high strength. Brass strip is made by combining copper with zinc, and is prized for its malleability and electrical conductivity. Cupronickel strip is made by combining copper with nickel, offering excellent resistance to corrosion, heat, and biofouling. These different types of copper alloy strips cater to a wide range of industries, including automotive, electrical, and marine sectors.</p></p>
