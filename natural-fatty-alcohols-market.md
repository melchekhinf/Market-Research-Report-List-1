<p><p>Natural fatty alcohols market encompasses two major types: saturated alcohols and unsaturated alcohols. Saturated alcohols are derived from saturated fatty acids and have single bonds between carbon atoms, resulting in a straight molecular structure. They exhibit properties like stability, emulsification, and thickening, making them suitable for use in skincare, personal care, and cleaning products. On the other hand, unsaturated alcohols are derived from unsaturated fatty acids and possess double bonds between carbon atoms, causing a bent molecular structure. They offer excellent moisturizing properties, making them ideal for use in cosmetics and hair care products.</p></p>
