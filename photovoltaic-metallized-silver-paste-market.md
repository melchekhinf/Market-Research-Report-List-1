<p><p>The photovoltaic metallized silver paste market is divided into two main types: front side silver paste and back side silver paste.  Front side silver paste is used for the front surface of solar cells, where it acts as a conductive layer to collect and transfer the generated electricity. It helps enhance the efficiency of solar cells by reducing the resistive losses. On the other hand, back side silver paste is applied on the back surface of solar cells to form a reflective layer, which prevents the loss of light and further enhances the overall performance of the solar cell.</p></p>
