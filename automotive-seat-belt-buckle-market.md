Three-point buckles, introduced in the late 1950s, provide better protection by securing the lap and shoulder areas. Four-point buckles offer additional security by adding a second strap to the lap and shoulder areas. Five-point buckles provide the highest level of safety by adding two straps connected to the hips. These buckles cater to different safety needs and can be found in various vehicles.</p></p>
