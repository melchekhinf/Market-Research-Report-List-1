<p><p>PVC pipes and fittings are categorized into different market types based on their shape and structure. K-type refers to the pipes and fittings that have a bell and spigot joint, allowing for easy installation and disassembly. L-type signifies pipes and fittings with a socket joint, providing a secure and leak-proof connection. M-type represents pipes and fittings with a male and female joint, enabling a reliable and tight connection. These market types offer various options for different applications, ensuring the PVC pipes and fittings cater to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
