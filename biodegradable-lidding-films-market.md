<p><p>Biodegradable lidding films refer to types of films that can decompose naturally over time, reducing environmental pollution. There are several types of biodegradable lidding films available in the market. PE type films are made from polyethylene and offer excellent clarity and sealing properties. PP type films are made from polypropylene, known for high temperature resistance and flexibility. PET type films are made from polyethylene terephthalate and provide good gas barrier properties. Other types of biodegradable lidding films include those made from renewable resources like PLA (polylactic acid) or those with additional functionalities like antifog or antimicrobial properties.</p></p>
