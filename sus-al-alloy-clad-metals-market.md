<p><p>In the SUS-Al-alloy Clad Metals market, there are two main types of structures: bilayer and three-layer structures. A bilayer structure consists of two layers, one made of SUS (stainless steel) metal and the other made of Al-alloy (aluminum alloy) metal. This type of structure offers enhanced corrosion resistance and mechanical strength. On the other hand, a three-layer structure includes an additional intermediate layer made of a bonding material between the SUS and Al-alloy layers. This structure provides better adhesion between the layers, improving overall durability and performance.</p></p>
