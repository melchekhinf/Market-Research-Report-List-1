<p><p>The Fiber Optic Cable Puller market consists of two types, namely Portable and Table-mounted pullers. Portable pullers are lightweight and easy to carry around, making them suitable for projects that require mobility. They are ideal for confined spaces or awkward positions where a table-mounted puller would not be suitable. On the other hand, Table-mounted pullers are designed for stability and ease of use on a workbench or table. They are preferred for larger projects or tasks that demand a fixed setup, allowing for efficient cable pulling. Both types cater to different requirements within the fiber optic cable installation industry.</p></p>
