EPS panels are lightweight and have good thermal insulation properties. PU panels offer excellent thermal and acoustic insulation. Rock Wool panels have fire-resistant properties and provide good sound insulation. Glass Wool panels are energy-efficient and offer thermal insulation. The Others category includes sandwich panels made from materials such as cement, calcium silicate, and perlite. These panels are used in various construction applications for their specific properties and advantages.</p></p>
