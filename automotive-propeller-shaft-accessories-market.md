<p><p>The automotive propeller shaft accessories market includes various types, such as slip joints and universal joints. A slip joint is a coupling device that allows for slight movement and misalignment between two shafts. It is commonly used in drive shaft applications. On the other hand, universal joints are used to transmit torque and motion between non-aligned shafts. They are ideal for applications where flexibility and torque transmission at varying angles are required, such as in automotive propeller shafts.</p></p>
