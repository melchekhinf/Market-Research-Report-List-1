<p><p>Bullet resistant auto glass is a specialized type of glass used to protect vehicles and their occupants from ballistic threats. There are four main market types of bullet resistant auto glass: Polycarbonate, Acrylic, Glass-Clad Polycarbonate, and Poly-Vinyl Butyral (PVB). Polycarbonate refers to a plastic material known for its high impact resistance. Acrylic refers to a transparent plastic material with good optical clarity. Glass-Clad Polycarbonate combines the strength of polycarbonate with the transparency of glass. Poly-Vinyl Butyral (PVB) refers to a synthetic resin used as an interlayer between glass layers to improve bullet resistance.</p></p>
