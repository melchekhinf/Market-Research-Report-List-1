<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Industrial Catalyst market?</strong></p>
<p><p>The global industrial catalyst market is witnessing several emerging trends. First, there is a growing demand for environmentally friendly catalysts, driven by regulations and increasing awareness of sustainability. Fourth, the development of nanotechnology has led to the emergence of nanocatalysts, offering higher efficiency and selectivity.
