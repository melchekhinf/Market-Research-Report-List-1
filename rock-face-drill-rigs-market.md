<p><p>The Rock Face Drill Rigs market encompasses three main types of drill rigs: single boom drilling rig, double boom drilling rig, and multi boom drilling rig. </p><p>A single boom drilling rig consists of a single drilling arm that can move up and down and rotate horizontally, allowing for efficient drilling in confined spaces. </p><p>A double boom drilling rig has two drilling arms, which enables simultaneous drilling on two different faces of the rock, increasing productivity. </p><p>A multi boom drilling rig has multiple drilling arms, allowing for drilling on several rock faces simultaneously, making it ideal for large-scale mining operations.</p></p>
