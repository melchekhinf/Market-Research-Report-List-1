<p><p>Copper Clad Laminate (CCL) is a composite material commonly used in the manufacturing of printed circuit boards (PCBs). It consists of a thin layer of copper foil bonded to a non-conductive substrate, such as fiberglass or resin. CCL provides the base material for PCBs, offering excellent electrical conductivity and mechanical strength.</p><p>Prepreg, on the other hand, is a composite material used in the fabrication of multi-layer PCBs. It is made by impregnating a substrate (usually fiberglass) with a resin, which is partially cured to form a sticky, flexible sheet. The prepreg serves as an insulator between copper layers, providing structural integrity and facilitating the lamination process during PCB assembly.</p><p>The Copper Clad Laminate (CCL) and Prepreg Market is projected to witness steady growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for electronic devices, such as smartphones, tablets, automotive electronics, and LED lighting, is driving the demand for CCL and prepreg. Additionally, the growing trend of miniaturization and advancements in technology requiring higher performance PCBs are further boosting the market.</p><p>Furthermore, the rise of 5G technology and the Internet of Things (IoT) is expected to create significant opportunities for the CCL and prepreg market. These technologies require high-speed and high-frequency PCBs, which demand specialized CCL and prepreg materials.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) worldwide is expected to fuel the demand for CCL and prepreg.
