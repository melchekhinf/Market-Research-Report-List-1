<p><ul><li>Passive Railway Wheel Sensor</li><li>Active Railway Wheel Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Train Wheel Magnetic Sensor Market includes two main types: Passive and Active Railway Wheel Sensors. Passive Railway Wheel Sensors are devices that generate electricity when in motion. They rely on the train's wheels passing by a stationary magnetic field, inducing an electrical current. Active Railway Wheel Sensors, on the other hand, require an external power source. They use magnetic or optical technology to sense the wheel's rotation and provide accurate measurements. Both types of sensors assist in monitoring train speed, wheel position, and helping ensure safe operation on railways.</p></p>
